## 1. Introduction

Infini provides merchants with secure, stable, and easy-to-integrate digital currency payment solutions.

When users complete on-chain payments, the system automatically monitors transactions, identifies receipts, and credits assets to the merchant's payment account in real-time, with no manual intervention required throughout the entire process.

Merchants can operate through API or merchant backend, including:

- Creating orders and initiating payments
- Viewing real-time order and payment status
- Initiating settlements or withdrawals
- Configuring Webhooks to receive order status change notifications


Infini helps merchants quickly build reliable digital currency payment capabilities, suitable for e-commerce, digital entertainment, SaaS services, cross-border business, system integrators, and various other scenarios.
